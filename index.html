Pixelorant

A 2D Valorant Experience

You are the last player standing! Help Sage find the bomb to defuse it. You have 1 minute or it will explode, killing all of the city Breeze. Beware of the enemy team passing by, they cannot touch you. Good luck!

Use the arrow keys to move and (se calhar) the mouse to point and space bar to shoot. The timer is displayed at the corner of the screen
